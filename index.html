About US

Qimmati

Expertly Crafted Solutions for a Cleaner and Fresher World

Our company was established in 2018 with the goal of contributing to the improvement of quality of life, inspired by our government's Vision 2030. We are dedicated to providing high-quality, locally manufactured products, supporting industrial localization, and playing a vital role in packaging, transportation, and distribution to achieve significant economic progress on a national level. The company's activities include the production of most consumer products needed by families, with a focus on safe and reliable products for cleaning floors, surfaces, and clothing, as well as offering premium personal and household care products.
